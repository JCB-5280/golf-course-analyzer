# Emerging Technologies to Leverage

| Technology | Use‑Case | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| **AI \u0026 ML** | Predictive maintenance, demand forecasting, personalized player coaching | Drives differentiation – *SmartCourse* shows AI‑driven insights |
| **IoT Sensors** | Edge devices for soil moisture, temperature, wind, turf health | Enables real‑time irrigation, reduces water waste |
| **Satellite/Drone Remote Sensing** | Large‑area terrain mapping, damage detection | *Groundsense* uses AI segmentation; provides high‑frequency updates |
| **Blockchain or Distributed Ledger** | Immutable audit trail for maintenance logs, tokenized memberships | Growing interest in secure records; some ESG platforms exploring this |
| **VR/AR** | Player coaching, virtual tours, immersive training courses | Emerging Niche; *Arccos* integrated AR for shot visualization |
| **Zero‑Trust Architecture** | For remote member access and staff devices | Industry best practice; reduces breach risk |

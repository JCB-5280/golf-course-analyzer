# Competitive Landscape Snapshot: Player Analytics

| Vendor | Strengths | Gaps / GCA Opportunity |
|---|---|---|
| **Arccos Golf** | Excellent automatic shot tracking; deep database. | Primarily descriptive; forecasting is basic and lacks TimesFM-level time-series accuracy. |
| **18Birdies** | Large social community; simple GPS/Scorecard. | Forecasting is a "premium" add-on with limited strategic depth; UI can be cluttered. |
| **Shot Scope** | No-subscription hardware model; solid data depth. | Lacks a high-interactivity visualization framework like Dash; predictive engine is underdeveloped. |
| **Sportsbox AI** | High-end 3D swing analysis from video. | Hyper-focused on the swing; lacks broad scoring trend forecasting and strategic modeling. |

*Opportunity:* Build the first **Predictive-First** player platform that moves beyond "what happened" to "what will happen." By integrating **TimesFM** for high-accuracy forecasting and **Dash** for interactive strategic modeling, GCA provides a pro-level analytics edge that descriptive apps cannot match.

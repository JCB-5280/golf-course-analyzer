## Interview Questions for Golf Course Analyzer Users

Below are a set of interview questions designed to help us understand user needs, pain points, and feature preferences for the Golf Course Analyzer product. These questions can be used in user interviews or surveys.

### User Background
1. **Can you tell us about your golfing experience?** (e.g., casual/recreational, competitive, coach, course manager)
2. **How often do you play or practice golf?** (frequency per week/month/season)

### Current Pain Points
3. **What aspects of your golf game currently lack useful feedback or analytics?**
4. **How do you currently track your performance?** (e.g., manual scorecards, mobile apps, wearables, video analysis, launch monitors)

### Feature Interest
5. **Which of the following potential features would be most valuable to you?** (Select all that apply)
   - Real‑time shot tracking and visualization
   - Detailed course mapping and hole‑by‑hole analytics
   - AI‑driven swing recommendations & improvement tips
   - Weather conditions integration and impact analysis
   - Equipment performance tracking (clubs, balls)
   - Competitive leaderboards and challenges
   - Integration with existing devices (GPS watches, launch monitors)
   - Community/social sharing of stats and achievements

6. **How frequently would you use a mobile/desktop application that provides these analytics?** (multiple times per round, after each round, weekly, monthly)
7. **What level of detail would you expect in the analytics?** (high‑level summary vs. per‑shot deep dive)

### Data & Privacy
8. **Are you comfortable sharing your location data during rounds for course mapping and analysis?**
9. **Would you be willing to upload video or sensor data for improved AI analysis?**

### Pricing & Business Model
10. **Which pricing model would you prefer?**
    - Monthly/annual subscription
    - One‑time purchase
    - Freemium with optional premium features
    - Pay‑per‑use (e.g., per round analysis)

### Integration & Ecosystem
11. **How important is integration with existing devices or platforms (e.g., GPS watches, launch monitors, existing golf apps)?**
12. **Do you use any other golf‑related software or services that you’d like this product to integrate with?**

### Community & Social Features
13. **How important is a community or social component (e.g., sharing stats with friends, challenges, leaderboards) to you?**

### Open‑Ended
14. **Do you have any additional ideas or features that you think would improve your golf experience?**

---
*These questions are intended to guide user interviews and help prioritize features for the Golf Course Analyzer product.*
